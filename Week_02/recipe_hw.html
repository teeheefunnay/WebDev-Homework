Banana Split


Descirption

Are you split between which ice cream you want to eat... well why not make a classic dessert that you can have it all! The more the merrier they always say!


Ingredients

  • Vanilla, Chocolate and Strawberry Ice Cream
  • 1 Whole Banana
  • 1 Cherry
  • Whip Cream
  • Chocolate Syrup

Instructions

  1. Peel and cut your banana in half lengthwise, laid in the dish
  2. Take a scoop of vanilla, chocolate and strawberry ice cream and place each scoop in between the split banana
  3. Top it with some whip cream and chocolate syrup
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
